2. Definitions

  • SwiftMart Platform: The e-commerce ecosystem including website, mobile applications, APIs, and associated services.
  • Seller/Vendor: A registered business or individual authorized to list and sell products/services on SwiftMart.
  • Buyer/Customer: Any registered or guest user purchasing products/services through SwiftMart.
  • Order: A legally binding contract formed when a buyer completes checkout for a seller's product.
  • Product: Any tangible good, digital product, or service listed for sale.
  • SwiftMart Wallet: The internal payment account where funds are held during transactions.
  • Dispute Resolution Center: SwiftMart's formal process for handling transaction conflicts.
  • Service Fees: Commissions and processing fees charged by SwiftMart for platform usage.

3. User Registration and Account Management

3.1 General Requirements

  • All users must provide accurate, current, and complete information during registration.
  • Users are responsible for maintaining the confidentiality of their account credentials.
  • Accounts are non-transferable and may not be sold or shared with others.

3.2 Seller-Specific Requirements

  • Must be at least 18 years old and legally authorized to operate a business in Kenya.
  • Required verification documents include:
    • Valid Kenyan National ID or Passport
    • KRA PIN Certificate
    • Business registration documents (for registered entities)
    • Recent utility bill or bank statement for address verification
  • Must maintain accurate inventory levels and promptly update unavailable items.
  • Agree to SwiftMart's Seller Code of Conduct and Performance Standards.

3.3 Buyer Requirements

  • Must provide valid contact information including verifiable phone number.
  • Responsible for ensuring delivery address accuracy and accessibility.
  • Must not engage in fraudulent activities including:
    • False chargebacks
    • Unauthorized transactions
    • Abuse of return policies

3.4 Account Suspension and Termination

SwiftMart reserves the right to suspend or terminate accounts that:

  • Violate these terms or applicable laws
  • Engage in fraudulent activities
  • Receive excessive complaints or negative feedback
  • Demonstrate poor performance metrics

4. Product Listing and Content Guidelines

4.1 Listing Requirements

  • Each product must have:
    • Clear, high-resolution images (minimum 800x800 pixels)
    • Accurate, descriptive title (max 100 characters)
    • Detailed description including specifications, materials, dimensions
    • Correct categorization
    • Transparent pricing in KES (including all applicable taxes)
    • Shipping options and costs
    • Return policy (minimum 7 days for defective items)
  • Stock levels must be updated within 24 hours of inventory changes.

4.2 Prohibited Items

The following items are strictly prohibited on SwiftMart:

  • Illegal drugs and drug paraphernalia
  • Weapons, ammunition, and explosives
  • Counterfeit or replica items
  • Stolen goods or property
  • Human remains or body parts
  • Hazardous materials
  • Adult content and services
  • Items promoting hate or violence
  • Government documents and IDs
  • Recalled or unsafe products

4.3 Intellectual Property

  • Sellers must own or have legal rights to sell all listed items.
  • Unauthorized use of trademarks, copyrights, or patented designs is prohibited.
  • SwiftMart will remove infringing content upon notification and may terminate repeat offenders.

4.4 Product Quality Standards

  • All products must meet Kenyan quality and safety standards.
  • Food items must have proper labeling including expiry dates.
  • Electronics must comply with KEBS regulations.

5. Pricing and Payments

5.1 Pricing Structure

  • Sellers set their own prices but must include all applicable taxes.
  • Price gouging during emergencies is prohibited.
  • SwiftMart may recommend competitive pricing but does not control final prices.

5.2 Payment Methods

SwiftMart supports multiple payment options:

  • M-PESA (Lipa Na M-PESA)
  • Debit/Credit Cards (Visa, Mastercard)
  • Bank Transfers
  • SwiftMart Wallet

5.3 Payment Processing

  • Buyer payments are held in escrow until order completion.
  • Sellers receive payment 7 days after delivery confirmation or after dispute resolution.
  • Processing fees are 2.5% for cards, 1% for mobile money, deducted from seller payout.

5.4 Commission Structure

Category Commission Rate

5.5 Refund Processing

  • Approved refunds are processed within 3-5 business days.
  • Original payment method refunds may take up to 14 days depending on financial institution.
  • Partial refunds may be issued for partially fulfilled orders.

6. Order Fulfillment and Logistics

6.1 Order Processing

  • Sellers must confirm orders within 24 hours.
  • Standard processing time is 1-3 business days unless otherwise specified.
  • Sellers must update order status throughout fulfillment process.

6.2 Shipping Options

SwiftMart offers multiple shipping solutions:

  • Seller Shipping: Vendor arranges own logistics
  • SwiftMart Logistics: Our integrated delivery network
  • Third-Party Partners: Approved courier services

6.3 Delivery Standards

  • Nairobi Metro: 1-2 business days
  • Major Towns: 2-4 business days
  • Remote Areas: 5-7 business days
  • Delivery attempts will be made twice before return to sender

6.4 Packaging Requirements

  • Items must be securely packaged to prevent damage.
  • Fragile items require special packaging.
  • Shipping labels must be clearly visible.

7. Returns, Refunds and Disputes

7.1 Return Policy Framework

  • 7-Day Return Window: For defective/wrong items
  • 3-Day Return Window: For change of mind (seller may specify restocking fee)
  • Non-Returnable Items: Perishables, intimate apparel, custom orders

7.2 Return Process

  1. Buyer initiates return request through platform
  2. Seller responds within 48 hours
  3. If approved, return instructions provided
  4. Seller inspects returned item
  5. Refund processed upon verification

7.3 Dispute Resolution

  • Mediation begins after 72 hours of no resolution between parties
  • SwiftMart may request:
    • Photographic evidence
    • Delivery confirmation
    • Product documentation
  • Decisions are binding and may involve:
    • Full or partial refunds
    • Order cancellations
    • Compensation

8. Seller Performance Metrics

8.1 Key Performance Indicators

  • Order Defect Rate: Must remain below 5%
  • Cancellation Rate: Must remain below 2%
  • Late Shipment Rate: Must remain below 4%
  • Response Time: Must be under 24 hours

8.2 Consequences for Poor Performance
